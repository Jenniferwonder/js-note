# Absolute length units (For print styles)

- Absolute length units (For print styles)
  - `cm` (centimeter)
  - `mm` (millimeter)
    - 10 mm = 1 cm
  - `in` (inch)
    - 2.54 cm = 1 inch
  - `pt` (point)
    - 72 pt = 1 inch
  - `pc` (pica)
    - 12 pt = 1 pc
  - `px`
    - the smallest unit on a screen
